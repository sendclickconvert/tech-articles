## Quick Fix Guide

If you're dealing with a corrupted PDF file, try these solutions in order:

### 1. Try a Different PDF Reader
Sometimes it's not the file that's corrupted - it's your reader having compatibility issues.

### 2. Use Adobe Online Repair Tool
Adobe offers a free online tool that can repair many corrupted PDFs. It's surprisingly effective.

### 3. Try Professional File Repair Software
For serious corruption, specialized recovery tools can often salvage your data.
